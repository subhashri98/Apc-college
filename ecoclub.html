Eco Club

Eco club is formed with the intent of creating knowledge about environment among students. It conducts programmes like competition, guest lecturers etc. Students from all the departments are participated and get benefit. The Eco club maintains a herbal garden inside the college and it has more than 35 rare medicinal plants.
