    Quiz Club

    In order to emphasize the competitive ability among the wards, Quiz Club is functioning in our college. The club members represent the college in almost all competitions throughout the University. Apart from conducting various quiz competitions in the college, the club helps the students to prepare for the competitive exams,by supplying resource materials, organizing groupdiscussions and providing guidance from the professions.

    Primary Objectives:

    • The main objective of quiz club is to popularize quizzing activities among students and to encourage them to take part in all competitive examination at State and National level.
    • To instil our students the values of knowledge, wisdom and compassion to make them smart citizens.
